# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese tax and finance practitioners, including accountants, tax advisors, finance staff, enterprise tax handlers, reviewers, and administrators. They use the product during task-focused policy lookup, answer verification, source review, and document governance work where stale policy or unsupported certainty can create real compliance risk.

## Product Purpose

Caishui WebApp is a Chinese tax knowledge-base Q&A system that answers from verified, retrievable, time-valid source material. Success means users can ask a tax question, receive a grounded answer with immutable citation snapshots, understand evidence limits, and inspect or govern the source documents that feed retrieval.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a professional tax research workspace: quiet enough for repeated daily use, clear about uncertainty, and explicit about evidence state.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page theatrics, decorative dashboards, over-saturated fintech styling, chat apps that hide sources, and any interface that implies the system has complete regulatory coverage. Do not make "not retrieved" look like "does not exist."

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before confidence: every generated answer state should make the strength and limits of evidence visible.
2. Task density with breathing room: practitioners need scan-friendly details, not a sparse landing page.
3. Trust through consistency: use one restrained component vocabulary across QA, review, upload, and history.
4. Locality of responsibility: UI surfaces present state, while domain modules own retrieval, policy, and persistence behavior.
5. Reversible governance by default: withdrawal, annotation, and audit states should feel first-class, not exceptional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Avoid color-only status communication. Support keyboard input for the main QA flow, keep focus states visible, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
